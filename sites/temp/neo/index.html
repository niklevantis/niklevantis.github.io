About

Born in Athens, Greece, Nick grew up in a fertile cultural and musical period expressing his artistic concerns in the early eighties. Computers and synths helped him to step deeper into the world of electronic music and digital art. Over three decades worked and played in several known and unknown music groups and projects.

Today Nick released from stereotypes and necessary standards creating music and trying to diversify overcoming the limits of specific genres and recycled ideas that increasingly arriving today in our ears mirroring the controlled and nasty subculture of globalisation.

The last three years has created forty-four audio tracks that without being assigned to a particular genre of music contain many primary avant-garde and minimalist elements.

They aim to travel the listener into the magical world of electronic music. A journey that in many cases will "scratch" your ears trying to be subversive and simultaneously romantic. You can find more songs on Soundcloud.
