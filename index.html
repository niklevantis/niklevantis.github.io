+-------> About

Nikos is creating pieces of music and is trying to   
differentiate  by surpassing the limits of  music     
genres. Recent activities include Paralogik project
with Eugene, live coding and visual experimentation.  

+-------> May 1 2020

Resurrected and remixed from an old low fi recording
of 2019 ,''in your eyes'' follows the dystopian society 
cruelty and finds hope in a world of a pandemic terror.
Voices and lead synths by Nick Levantis,Bass synth, 
dr.machine, and sound mixing by Gene B. @Weird Sounds 
Studio
