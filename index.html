ABOUT

Born in Athens, Greece, Nick grew up in a fertile cultural and musical period at the early eighties. Computer programming studies and synths knowledge helped him to step deeper into the world of electronic music and digital art.

Over the years he has played and collaborated with many other musicians experimenting through different styles.Today Nick is creating pieces of music trying to differentiate and surpass the limits of specific genres.

The last two years has created a total of forty four genre agnostic songs which contain many avant-garde and minimalist elements that are meant to travel the listener to the magical world of electronic music. You can find full list of Nick songs on Soundcloud.
