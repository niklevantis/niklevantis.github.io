Nick Levantis grew up in a fertile cultural and musical period in the early eighties. Programming and synths helped him to step deeper into the world of electronic music and computer art. Over three decades he has played and collaborated with many other musicians experimenting through different styles. Nick is creating pieces of music and is trying to differentiate and surpass the limits of specific genres. You can listen full list of Nick songs on Soundcloud. The latest project is the experimental electronic band Paralogik which created together with Eugene Broumidis. Their dark electronic sound travels through ethereal and nostalgic melodies with lyrics that touch many unresolved social concerns.Τheir first live performance will take place on9 December 2016 in Athens.
